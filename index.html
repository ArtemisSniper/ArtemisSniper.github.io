FAQ

What Operating Systems does Artemis support?

Artemis is compatible with Windows, Linux and Mac.

What are the requirements to run Artemis?

Python 3.8 or above is required for running Artemis.

How can I calculate my delay?

Using the Auto Delay feature can give you a rough estimate of your delay, which can then be further adjusted/tuned. To get the perfect delay, test by looking at the droptime and when the requests were received. Then you can adjust your delay. Increasing it will send the requests earlier while decreasing the delay will send them later.
